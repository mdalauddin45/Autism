Mild (Lower Likelihood of Autism)

Treatment:

  • Skill Development Workshops: Participate in workshops focused on improving social and communication skills.
  • Counseling: Attend occasional counseling sessions to address any specific concerns or social anxieties; consultation with an autism specialist is generally not necessary at this level.
  • Mindfulness and Relaxation Techniques: Engage in practices like mindfulness and relaxation techniques to manage stress and enhance self-awareness.

Education:

  • Mainstream Education: Continue in mainstream education settings with occasional support as needed.
  • Skill-Building Programs: Enroll in programs that focus on enhancing soft skills and social interactions within the mainstream environment.
  • Inclusive Learning: Ensure inclusive classroom settings that promote diversity and understanding of different learning styles.

Community and Social Support:

  • Social Clubs and Groups: Join social clubs or groups that align with personal interests to build social networks.
  • Peer Support Networks: Engage with peer support networks that offer guidance and shared experiences.
  • Volunteer Opportunities: Participate in community service or volunteer opportunities to build connections and social skills.
