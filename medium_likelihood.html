Medium (Moderate Likelihood of Autism)

Treatment:

  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Engage in CBT to develop coping strategies for social interactions and anxiety; consider consulting an autism specialist for tailored therapy.
  • Social Skills Training: Attend regular social skills training sessions to improve communication and interaction abilities.
  • Occupational Therapy: Use occupational therapy to develop practical skills for daily living and enhance sensory integration.

Education:

  • Individualized Education Plan (IEP): Develop an IEP in a mainstream school to provide specific accommodations and support.
  • Resource Rooms: Utilize resource rooms or specialized support within the mainstream school for additional help.
  • Autism-Friendly Programs: Consider programs that offer a blend of mainstream and specialized instruction to meet individual needs.

Community and Social Support:

  • Support Groups: Join autism support groups for individuals and families to share experiences and resources.
  • Mentorship Programs: Participate in mentorship programs with individuals who have similar experiences.
  • Social Events: Attend organized social events that are structured and supportive of diverse social needs.
